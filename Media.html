    THE COLLAPSE OF GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE IN KOGI STATE





































    Kogi state has being in the news for the wrong reasons for several years now, particularly in this 4th Republic. Sadly, the peak of this quagmire, was at its highest ebb, under the current administration.

    In my over 15years of partnering with state governments of different states of the federation. I have never in my entire life, seen a state utmostly governed by just two persons, thereby relegating to the background, the role of other critical stakeholders such as the Commissioners, Permanent Secretaries, Local Government administrators, Directors in various Ministries, Head of various parastatals , just to mention a few.

    In this dispensation, the governor and his chief of staff, whose role ought to begin and end as the Head of staff in the government house, have consistently been seen, playing the role of these other critical stakeholders. This alone, is a sign of a failure in governance and if this is allowed to continue, we will wake up someday and realize, the dreams of our founding fathers have only met its Waterloo, and have ended in the dungeon of oblivion.

    What we all need at the moment is a visionary leader, who will not use his office to undermine the role of other critical stakeholders, but work to ensure their strengthening, for the overall institutional growth and development of these Ministries, Departments and Agencies (MDAs), which will translate into an overall growth of the state and nation at large.

    If Kogi must move forward, we must all understand that we've got our roles to play, to ensure its actualization. This is the mindset towards which we all should be driven by, as we approach the governorship election in November 2019, whose process has already commenced.

    God bless Kogi state
    God bless Nigeria , our dear nation.
