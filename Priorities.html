    SECURITY

    Establishment of Kogi State Security Services (KSSS) to ensure lives and properties are secured in all Kogi state communities Within my first 100 days in the office, a 1,000 security personnel in 100 communities would be engaged, properly trained and empowered to help address security - related issues and curtail crimes in various neighborhoods. This initiative would be in partnership with existing Security Agencies within the State. With this, we would be creating over 1, 000 jobs for our youths, thus, reducing number of unemployed youths in the state.

    EDUCATION

    One Government-run school in each of the 3 Senatorial Districts will be transformed into a standard learning environment by renovating the classrooms and equipping them with modern learning and teaching aids for both students and teachers. In the same vein, teachers of these model schools would be properly trained and motivated on the need for continuous personal development.

    TEACHERS

    All unduly sacked or retrenched teachers would be recalled and properly equipped before they are engaged to teach the pupils. We would have commenced payment of salary arrears. The first phase of "Capacity building of teachers and Non teaching staff would have commenced tagged TRAIN THE TRAINERS and LEARN TO IMPROVE (Non teaching staff) . To commence implementation of the 26% Budget for education in our first budget.

    THE CHILDREN/STUDENTS

    Free education for UBE. Primary to Junior Secondary School. Scholarship will be introduced for the best `100 students in the Senior Secondary School, in 100 Schools within the State.

    INFRASTRUCTURAL DEVELOPMENT

    Each of the 3 Senatorial district will have any of the 5 basic infrastructural needs in place, based on the leverage point.Amongst which are:

    Power installation

    • Construction of central electric water plant that can be distributed within a large area.
    • Construction of Roads and drainages
    • Revamp of the cottage hospitals, provision of Primary Health Centers for rural areas
    • Purchase of educational materials

    APPOINTMENTS

    2 nominees will be presented to Kogites to choose their favorite through an online survey, for appointments into various MDAs (Ministries, Departments and Agencies

    *MINERAL RESOURCES*

    A 10-man committee of Kogites from various professions will be contracted to research all economic viable mineral resources within the state and its benefit to Kogites for immediate extraction.
