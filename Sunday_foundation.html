    Vision & Mission

    To ensure by 2050, 80% of Nigerian communities have access to the five fundamental needs of man. Amongst which are good infrastructure, portable water, constant power, quality education and sound healthcare delivery system.

    &

    To advocate for good governance, train, empower young entrepreneurs and engage in community development services.

    Preamble

    Sunday Bala Oma Foundation is a Non Profit organization, registered with Corporate Affairs Commission Headquarters, on the 15th of November 2013, with registration number CAC/IT/NO 65486. The organization is saddled with the responsibility of advocating for good governance and qualitative education in Nigeria.
    In the last five years, the organization has impacted more than 24,000 Nigerians across 7 states of the Federation. These states are Kogi, Bauchi, Benue, Plateau, Bayelsa, Nasarawa and the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja. The organization is involved in activities such as organizing annual Seminars, Workshops, Secondary school Essay competitions, visit to orphanage homes, provision of technical support for Internally displaced persons camps, award of excellence to deserving nationalists, annual summer entrepreneurship programmes, just to mention a few.
